José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda

National Hero of the Philippines

1431 - Sighisoara - Vlad III Dracula is born

1861-1870: Informal education by mother

1861: He was born from an Inquilino family

1870: He received formal education in Binan for 2 years

1872: Execution of GOMBURZA

1872: Imprisonment of Dona Teodora

1872: Rizal entered Ateneo

1872-1877: Rizal's Ateneo days

1877-1882: Philosophy of letters and Medicine at UST

1877-1882: Studied Land Surveying in Ateneo

1882: 1st travel to europe

1882-1885: Licensate in Philosophy of Letters

1883: He went to Paris for vacation

1887-1888: Back to Philippines and was involved in land dispute

1887: Publication of Noli me tangere in Berlin

1888: 2nd travel to europe to annotate De morga's work

1889: Celebration of Universal Exposition in Paris

1890: Expulsion of farmers from Hacienda de Calamba

1891: Publication of El Filibusterismo in Belgium

1892-1896: Exile in Dapitan

1892: Hongkong, to build a colony in North Borneo

1892: Founded La Liga Filipina in Philippines

1896: Execution of Rizal in Bagumbayan
